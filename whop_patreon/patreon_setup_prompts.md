## STEP 7 — Connect Discord (Auto Role Assignment)
**Dashboard → Settings → Integrations → Discord**

1. Click **Connect to Discord**
2. Authorise with your Discord account
3. Select your **Ascent Terminal** server
4. Map tiers to roles:

| Patreon Tier | Discord Role |
|--------------|--------------|
| Observer     | Observer     |
| Runner       | Runner       |
| Developer    | Developer    |

5. Save. Roles are now assigned and removed automatically.

---

## STEP 8 — Payout Setup
**Dashboard → Settings → Payouts**

- Add your bank account or PayPal
- Patreon pays out monthly (around the 5th of the month)
- UK creators: Patreon will ask for tax information — fill it in honestly
